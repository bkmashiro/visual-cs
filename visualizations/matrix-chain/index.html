How It Works

Recurrence:
m[i][i] = 0
For i < j:
m[i][j] = min over k in [i, j):
  m[i][k] + m[k+1][j]
  + p[i-1]·p[k]·p[j]

where p[i-1]·p[k]·p[j] is the cost of multiplying the two resulting matrices.

Fill order: diagonal by diagonal — all chains of length 2 first, then 3, etc.

Traceback: use the split table s[i][j] recursively to reconstruct the optimal parenthesization.

Complexity: O(n³) time, O(n²) space.
