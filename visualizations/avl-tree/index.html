How it works

AVL trees maintain a balance factor of -1, 0, or 1 for every node. After each insertion or deletion, the tree updates heights bottom-up and applies one of four rotations: LL, RR, LR, or RL.

  • Green nodes are currently balanced.
  • When rebalancing happens, the affected nodes pulse red and the rotation type appears in the overlay.
  • The canvas supports pan and zoom so deeper trees stay easy to inspect.
